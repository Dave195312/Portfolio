Hobbies

Here's a few of my hobbies:

Cooking

I love cooking, baking and generally creating food for my family and friends.

Whether it's a celebration dinner or a midweek meal or a Saturday afternoon baking session, I love creating and presenting good food.

DiY

I learned a lot as an engineering apprentice, and to this day I still use a lot of those skills in DIY.

I can fix a leaking tap, I can point your brickwork, I can plaster your ceiling and I'm a pretty good woodworker and carpenter! I really enjoy working on projects around the house; but I do know when to call in the professionals!

Interests

Maths and Science

I'm an avid reader, with a specific interest in non-fiction.

In particular, I read books on mathematical or scientific subjects, particularly Physics.

History

I failed History at GCE level when I was 15. I got a Grade 9 - the lowest possible.

It's not that I wasn't interested in history - it's just that I answered the questions that were meant for a different level.

Anyway, history - particlarly the History of Europe from the 14th Century - is one of my greatest interests.
